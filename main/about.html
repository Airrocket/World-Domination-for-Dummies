About Us

Game Design by Idiots is a groups of friends that decided to make some web games, and this is our website.

This project originally started when we were playing a board game on the last day of 7th grade. (Unfortunately none of us remember what it was called.) We didn't pay attention to the rules and turned it into "Global Communism Simulator". We started out just wanting to make this game, but then expanded our goal to include making our own website as well as some other games.

And this is that project.
